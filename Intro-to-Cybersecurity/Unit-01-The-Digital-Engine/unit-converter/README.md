# Unit Converter

**Category:** Intro to Cybersecurity  
**Difficulty:** Easy  
**Points:** 75

## Description

Your SOC team intercepted three suspicious file downloads in the last hour. The alert system logged them in different units. Your job is to convert all three to **Bytes**, identify which download is the largest, and submit its converted size.

Review the alert log and find the biggest file.

**Flag format:** `cyber{size_in_bytes}`
