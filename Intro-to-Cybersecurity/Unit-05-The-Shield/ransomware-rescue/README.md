# Ransomware Rescue

**Category:** Intro to Cybersecurity  
**Difficulty:** Hard  
**Points:** 300

## Description

🚨 EMERGENCY — A STUDENT HAS BEEN HIT BY RANSOMWARE 🚨

...sort of.

Your classmate Marcus left his laptop unlocked during lunch. When he came back, every file was "encrypted" and there was a **ransom note** on his desktop from a hacker group called **THE HOMEWORK DESTROYERS**.

They're demanding... well, you'll see.

The good news: these hackers are clearly amateurs. They left their **exact encryption method** right in the ransom note (because apparently they're proud of it).

You have two files:

- `ransom_note.txt` — the note left on his desktop (read this FIRST)
- `encrypted_flag.txt` — the encrypted flag you need to decode

Read the ransom note carefully. It tells you **exactly** how they encrypted the flag. Then reverse the process to decode `encrypted_flag.txt`.

**Flag format:** `cyber{decoded_message}`
